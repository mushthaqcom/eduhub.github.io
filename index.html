About Eduhub Academy

Dedicated to providing world-class education and nurturing the next generation of leaders

Our Mission

Founded in 2014 by Thariq Faiz, Eduhub Academy has been providing exceptional educational experiences from Grade 6 to A/L and beyond. We offer quality education in both English and Sinhala medium for students in Kandy City.

In 2025, we expanded with Eduhub International School, now serving over 50 students with our innovative approach that combines traditional academic excellence with modern teaching methodologies.
